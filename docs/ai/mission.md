# HalfThePrice Platform Mission

## Core Mission Statement
> **"HalfThePrice is a licensed Iraqi marketplace that only publishes live listings priced at or below 50% of verified retail evidence."**

## Regulatory Context & Compliance
The platform aligns with the latest Iraqi e-commerce directives from the Ministry of Trade and implementing electronic signature regulations. Every technical constraint is tied to these legal mandates:

1. **E-commerce Licensing:** Under Iraqi Trade Regulation, operating an online marketplace requires a valid license from the Ministry of Trade. The platform workflow enforces named trader verification before listings can go public.
2. **Consumer Disclosure:** Traders are required to clearly publish their business identity, contact information, returns/exchange policy, and complaint-handling procedures.
3. **Data Security & Privacy:** Encryption is mandatory for all user details and active transactional flows. A secure consent log tracks any third-party data processing.
4. **Electronic Payment Regulation:** Prepaid transactions follow Central Bank of Iraq (CBI) guidelines, utilizing licensed payment facilitators (ZainCash, QiCard, FastPay). COD remains the default exchange fallback.
